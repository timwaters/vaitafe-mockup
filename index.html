Help us collect data on river water health

We are looking for volunteers to help us collect data on river water health. This data will help us to better understand the impact of human activity on our rivers and to develop strategies for improving water quality.
As a volunteer, you can learn hpw to collect water samples and test for a variety of things.
Your participation in this project is important because it will help us to get a better understanding of the state of our rivers and to make a difference in protecting our environment.
