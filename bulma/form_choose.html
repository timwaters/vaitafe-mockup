Choose either Physical, Chemical or Biological

Physical

Choose this for collecting data about the physical characteristics of the waterway.
e.g. water temperature, clarity, flow, pH, turbidity, conductivity.

Chemical

pH (Acidity) Nitrates, Phosphates, Total dissolved solids (TDS)
Hardness: Use a hardness test kit to measure the hardness of the water.
Chlorine: Use a chlorine test kit to measure the chlorine levels in the water.

Biological

Water quality: Observe the water quality by looking for signs of pollution, such as oil, trash, or dead fish.
Water organisms: Collect water samples and identify the organisms present.
Water plants: Identify the water plants present.

Tips:
  • Be sure to collect data from the same location and time each time.
  • Collect data over a period of time to see how the water quality changes.
  • Be careful not to contaminate the water sample.
  • Be sure to clean and sanitize your equipment after each use.
  • Have fun!
